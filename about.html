About Me

I’m a tech enthusiast from Sweden, passionate about creating applications and websites from scratch. Growing up, I explored programming and media technology, which sparked my love for problem-solving and innovation.

To further pursue my interests, I moved to Sydney to continue my studies and expand my skills. Here, I enjoy experimenting with new tools and approaches, applying creativity and curiosity to every project I work on.

Outside of technology, I enjoy handcrafts such as embroidery, knitting, and crocheting. These hobbies keep my creativity flowing and inspire fresh perspectives in my coding projects, helping me approach challenges with patience and focus.

I am driven by curiosity, dedication, and a desire to learn. My goal is to combine technical skill with creativity to build meaningful, functional, and thoughtful experiences.
