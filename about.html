About Me

I am a Swedish Software Developer based in Sydney, driven by a lifelong fascination with building digital solutions from the ground up. My background in media technology, combined with my current studies, has shaped me into a developer who thrives at the intersection of technical logic and creative problem-solving.

I approach coding with the same mindset as my favorite handcrafts—embroidery, knitting, and crocheting. These hobbies have instilled in me a deep sense of patience, a sharp eye for detail, and the discipline required to build complex structures one "stitch" at a time. I believe that clean, maintainable code is a craft in itself.

With a focus on mobile app development and a curiosity for emerging tech, my goal is to create meaningful, functional experiences that are as thoughtful in their architecture as they are in their user interface.
