Overview

CraftRunner is an Android app that helps hobby enthusiasts keep track of craft supply prices. With a simple, user-friendly interface and Firebase login for secure access, the app lets you quickly browse a curated list of items. It’s designed to make comparing supplies effortless, helping you plan your next project with ease and stay inspired.

Quick Price Overview

See all craft supply prices at a glance, so you can make fast and informed decisions.

Secure Login

Sign in with Firebase for a simple and safe way to keep your app experience personal.

Easy Browsing

Navigate through a curated list of items effortlessly, making it easy to find what you need for your next project.

Takeaways

Building CraftRunner gave me my first hands-on experience with Android development in Java. I learned how to structure an app, manage user login with Firebase, and present information in a clean, user-friendly interface. Even as a beginner, I gained practical insight into app design, navigation, and creating a polished, functional Android app from start to finish.
